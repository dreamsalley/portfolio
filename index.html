ABOUT CSDG

CreativeStar Dezign Group is a design firm based near Washington DC, founded in 2009, dedicated to having passionate and innovative
ideas while retaining a fundamental and professional approach to graphic design.
The firm specializes in company branding and promotional items for artists, businesses and organizations.
It is a strong belief that each client should have a hand in their design solution and teamwork is the best approach
to the message each client desires to convey.
The firm offers professional, quality design solutions at very affordable prices for any budget. All work is customized to fit your specific needs.
